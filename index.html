Soviet Space Dogs Flight Museum

The Main Aspects covered by the below Visualization is the flight details and result of flights In which the soviet dogs have travelled.

Data Description: The data used in the below visualization is flight data, it contains date and name of flights that took the dogs to space, dogs that went in the respective flight, result of the trip and notes regarding the trip

Marks: Rocket image
Channel: Horizontal Distance, Max vertical distance moved by the Image, Animation of Rocket Image, Color on the Image.
Interaction: Mouse Over
Description of Visualization:- This is a static Visualization. The Attributes covered in visualization are Date of Departure, Max Altitude reached by the flight, Result of the trip, Number of Dogs Survived in the trip. The Rockets are placed along the x-axis based on date of departure of the flight. If the flight is animated to max altitude and back, It signifies successful completion of trip, If the Flight stays stationary, it represents flight got destroyed in the middle of trip. Number of blue colors on the flight represent number of dogs survived the trip. Maximum altitude reached by the flight during animation is the maximum altitude reached by the flight during the trip. Rocket name and dogs travelled in the rocket can be viewed by mouse over
